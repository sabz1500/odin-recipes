How to make delicious Salad

Salad Recipe:

Ingredients

  • 1 large head cabbage, cored and finely shredded
  • 1 medium onion, thinly sliced
  • 3/4 cup vinegar
  • 1/3 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons celery seed
  • 1 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon prepared mustard
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Directions

  1. Toss cabbage and onion together in a large bowl; set aside.
  2. Combine sugar, vinegar, oil, celery seed, salt, mustard, and pepper in a small saucepan; bring to a boil and simmer, stirring frequently, until sugar is dissolved, about 3 minutes.
  3. Let mixture cool slightly, about 5 minutes, then pour over cabbage mixture; toss well to coat. Cover and refrigerate at least 2 hours.
